LHS Winter Guard Timeline:

Year: Significance:
2013 Winter Guard starts as part of the Lincoln High School Indoor Drumline group
2018
  • Winter Guard decides to branch off and form a competitive group
  • In their first year in competition, they achieve 7th place in the only competition they attend, missing 5th place by only half a point
  • Show Title: "Reverie" - Song: "Reverie" by Debussy
    A gravedigger in a cemetary is visited by the ghosts from the graves he's diggin nearby, and they spend the evening cavorting - by morning he has fallen in love with one and when she must return to her grave, he dies of a broken heart and joins her in the afterlife.
2019
  • Due to Covid-19, the second year of competition is cut short before the Winter Guard is able to perform at a competition, although they perform at several basketball games
  • Show Title: "Somebody to Love" - Song: "Somebody to Love" by Queen
    On the season finale of "The Bachelor", our bachelor must decide between four finalists, but ultimately chooses a different path altogether - one of the two cameramen.
2020
  • Due to the continuing pandemic, the Winter Guard takes the season to try something different and instead has solo performances
2021
  • The Winter Guard gains enough funding to be able to have a complete competitive season - three competitions and the chance to compete at Championships. Come join the team and make history...
